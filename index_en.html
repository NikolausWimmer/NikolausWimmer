Profile

Experienced lecturer in statistics, quantitative methods, and research practice. Currently teaching at the University of Vienna, focusing on applied data analysis and supporting students in research methodology and implementation.

  • Research Practice: Developing questions, collecting data, presenting results

I believe in clear structure, accessible language, and actively engaging students. My teaching connects theoretical grounding with practical application – always with the goal of building research competence and critical thinking.
